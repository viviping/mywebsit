Restaurant

Situation 1 : 예약 확인

A : Do you have a reservation?

B : We have a reservation under the name of Cooper.

A : Right this way.

Situation 2 : 인원 확인

A : Welcome. How many in your party?

B : A table for three, please.

A : If you want a table inside, you'll have to wait for about 20 minutes. Would that be okay?

B : Oh, Yes. By all means.

Situation 3 : 메뉴

A : Can I see a menu, please?

B: Here you go. Take your time, I'll be back.
